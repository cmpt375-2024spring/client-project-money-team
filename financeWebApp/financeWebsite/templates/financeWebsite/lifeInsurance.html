Life Insurance

In this comprehensive YouTube video, it dives into Life insurance policies, which are contracts between an individual and an insurance company, where the insurer agrees to pay a designated beneficiary a sum of money upon the death of the insured person. In return, the insured pays a premium, either regularly or as a lump sum, and these policies are often used to provide financial security for family members or settle the insured's debts and expenses after passing.
