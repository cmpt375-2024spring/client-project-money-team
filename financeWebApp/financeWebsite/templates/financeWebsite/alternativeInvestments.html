Alternative Investments

In this comprehensive YouTube video, we dive into alternative investments. They are financial assets that do not fall into the conventional investment categories of stocks, bonds, and cash. These include a wide range of assets like real estate, hedge funds, private equity, commodities, and collectibles, and are often used to diversify an investment portfolio due to their low correlation with traditional markets.
